Designated_WEAs_25_07_29.zip

Description: 
This data package contains a shapefile of the Designated Wind Energy Areas (WEAs) for Nova Scotia. The WEA shapefile has been attributed with the WEA name (WEA) and approximate area in kilometers squared for each WEA (Area_km2).
